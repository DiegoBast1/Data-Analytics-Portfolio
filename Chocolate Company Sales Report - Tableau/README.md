# Chocolate Company Sales Report with Tableau

## Project Purpose
Business Summary Report where the purpose is to know the Top 10 Sales Persons by Country and key business information such as Sales Amount, Boxes Shipped and Shipment Count.

## Data sources
sample-data .xlsx file

## Tools Used
- Excel 
- Tableau 

## Key Insights Found
- Top 10 Sales Persons by Country (Interactive on Tableau)
- Sales by Country
- Sales Amount, Boxes Shipped and Shipment Count
- Recommend knowledge-sharing sessions for underperforming Salespersons.
